Our History

It all began third grade.

Addison and Kayla have been studying music since third grade. The Appoquinimink School District is the educator for all their musical talent. Kayla has been playing cello for roughly 11 years, while Addison has played piano for about ten years.


In November 2018, these like minded minds came together and created the idea of the "Hummingbirds" business. Through Addison's and Kayla's passion of music, they wanted to create an unforgettable experience for people through beautiful music. It is becoming a reality now, and we hope to bring you beautiful memories through the power of music. Feel free to download any sheet music, as I give access to the sheets for you to play.
